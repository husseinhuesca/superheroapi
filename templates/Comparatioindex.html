Correlations between six powers of 731 super heroes

The power statistic contains six powers; Combat, Intelligence, Durability, Speed, Power and Strength.

These six powers are grouped between two groups. The first group contains; Power, Combat and Durability. and the second group contains; Intelligence, Strength and Speed.

The strongest correlation is between combat and intelligence = 0.768 and the weakest correlation is between combat & strength = 0.303.
